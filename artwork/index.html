Robert O'Connor

Artist

Robert O'Connor, an American artist, is native of Brooklyn, New York and currently resides in Mystic, Connecticut. He studied drawing at the Brooklyn Museum of Art during his grammar school years then later at Brooklyn College and recently at the Rhode Island School of Design.

He was a student of the late Joseph Stapleton, formerly of Pratt Institute in Brooklyn, New York and The Art Students League in New York City.

He is an Elected Artist of the Mystic Arts Center in Mystic, Connecticut and has exhibited his work at the Dolan Art Gallery in Locust Valley, New York, the Oyster Bay Historical Society in Oyster Bay, New York, The Hempsted Houses in New London, Connecticut and the Ashbel Woodward House in Franklin, Connecticut.

His work is held in private collections in North America and Europe.

An on-line catalog of work is currently under construction.
